About Xplore Riyadh

Your gateway to Saudi Arabia's entertainment revolution. Inspired by Vision 2030, we help you discover the best events, sports, dining, and cultural experiences transforming Riyadh into a global destination.

Our Story & Vision 2030

Our journey is inspired by Saudi Vision 2030. Before 2016, entertainment options were limited. Today, thanks to the massive transformation led by the Kingdom, Saudi Arabia has become a global hub for tourism and leisure. We created Xplore Riyadh to keep up with this incredible speed of change and help you navigate the thousands of events happening every year.

The General Entertainment Authority (GEA)

We closely follow the calendar set by the General Entertainment Authority (GEA). The GEA has successfully organized massive seasons like Riyadh Season and Jeddah Season, attracting millions of visitors. Our goal is to organize this information for you in one simple, easy-to-use place.

Quality of Life Program

We believe in the "Quality of Life" program, a key pillar of Vision 2030. This program aims to improve lifestyles for individuals and families. By listing sports events, art exhibitions, concerts, and more, we encourage everyone to go out, participate, and enjoy life in the Kingdom.

What You Can Find Here

We provide accurate details about:

  • Mega Seasons: Updates on Riyadh Season, Jeddah Season, and Diriyah Season.
  • Concerts: Dates and ticket information for local and international artists.
  • Sports: Football matches, Formula 1, boxing championships, and esports.
  • Dining: Luxury restaurants, pop-up cafés, and culinary experiences.

Why This Matters

The entertainment sector creates jobs for Saudi youth and boosts the economy. By using Xplore Riyadh, you're supporting this growth while discovering the hidden gems of our beautiful country.
